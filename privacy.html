Privacy Policy

This policy explains how privacy is handled on VU Exam Preparation.

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Information We Collect

This website may store basic study activity in your browser, such as selected answers, test progress, theme preference, or assistant messages. This helps the app work smoothly during your practice session.

Personal Information

We do not require users to create an account or submit personal information to use the practice tests. If you contact us, we may use the information you provide only to respond to your message.

How We Use Information

Any locally stored information is used to improve your study experience, keep your preferences, and support quiz features. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Third-Party Services

The website may use external services such as fonts, hosting, analytics, or AI assistance. These services may process limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We aim to keep the website safe and reliable, but no online service can guarantee complete security. Avoid sharing sensitive personal, financial, or password information through the assistant or contact forms.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when the website changes. The updated date at the top of this page will show when the latest version was published.
